Joy Inspired Daily Objectives as follows

To be a leading advocate for the welfare and empowerment of widows and children, ensuring they live dignified and fulfilling lives.

  • We demonstrate empathy and kindness towards widows and children.
  • We operate with transparency, accountability, and honesty.
  • We value the dignity and worth of every widow and child. We strive to equip widows and children with the skills and resources they need to thrive.

To provide holistic support to widows and children, empowering them to overcome poverty, inequality, and social injustice. We will establish a robust monitoring and evaluation framework to track our progress, assess our impact, and make data-driven decisions.

Services

We render a variety of services to our numerous beneficiaries

Payment of School Fees for Widows' Children

Provide educational support to children, ensuring they access quality education and break the cycle of poverty.

Empowerment of Widows and Children

Equip widows and children with skills, knowledge, and resources to improve their economic and social well-being.

Welfare for Widows and their Children

Provide emotional, psychological, and material support to widows and children, ensuring they receive the care and assistance they need.

Catering for Gender-based Violence Survivors

Offer specialized support services to survivors of gender-based violence, including counseling, medical care, and legal assistance.
